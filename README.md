## Profile

I'm a physicist and computer scientist. I founded and lead the technical side of **Reservity AI**, a platform that automates bookings and client interactions for small and medium-sized businesses through conversational AI. My work starts from a simple premise: the models and math you study in graduate school don't have to stay there — they can become tools that any business uses every day to stop losing appointments and clients.

Before founding the company, I went through applied physics, AI research applied to oncology, and I'm now pursuing my master's at **INAOE**, deepening my focus on computing and artificial intelligence. That mix — mathematical rigor plus real product — is the thread running through everything I build.

## Research: AI for Early Skin Cancer Detection

I led a software and AI research project within the **Comprehensive Oncology Center at Hospital Ángeles Puebla**.

* **Goal** — Design and train an algorithm capable of processing dermatological images and data to support early skin cancer detection.
* **Clinical application** — Equip the oncology team with a high-precision predictive tool to help identify anomalies or malignant lesions before they become invasive.

This project not only validated my ability to solve computer vision and pattern recognition problems on real, sensitive data; it also laid the groundwork in automated data processing that I later carried into the commercial world when founding Reservity AI.

## Reservity AI — AI-Powered Business Automation

Reservity AI connects conversational AI with the channels where a business's clients already are, closing appointments and bookings without human intervention.

### Natural Language Processing
The core of the product. Systems capable of interpreting complex human intent within a conversation to schedule, confirm, and reschedule appointments autonomously — without the business having to write a single rigid decision-tree flow.

### Omnichannel API Integration
A centralized architecture that connects messaging flows — **Meta API for WhatsApp, Instagram, and Messenger** — with automated databases and cloud CRM, so the business sees all its conversations and bookings in one place regardless of the originating channel.

### Stochastic Models & Schedule Optimization
My background in applied physics translates here into efficient algorithms for handling schedules, availability, and automated waiting queues, reducing the margins of error typical of manual scheduling.

## How I Build

1. **Math first, product second.** A well-posed model is worth more than ten rushed integrations.
2. **AI is only useful if a real business can use it without a manual.** The goal isn't technical sophistication for its own sake — it's a small business that stops losing clients.
3. **Scientific rigor, applied without pretense.** I come from research in health and physics, and I bring that to solving everyday business problems.

Always open to connecting with other founders, researchers working on AI applied to health, and anyone who wants to talk about computing, physics, or business automation.
